About Superior Smart Home

My name is Vipul Bansal. I am currently a junior at Walton High School and founded this company during my sophomore year at Walton. I always heard people asking for help when deciding what smart devices to buy, what security systems are the most effective, and overall how to have a smarter and safer home. I founded this company to help with just that, making all the homes that I can smarter and safer.

I also realized that many traditional smart home businesses charge hundreds of dollars per hour. I wanted to make smart home and security devices as accessible as possible, so I decided to charge only $50/hour and help my community get the best deals on the devices they buy, taking advantage of the best sales and deals that I can find.
